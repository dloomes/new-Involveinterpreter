# Involve Interpreter Portal — Customer User Guide

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal
Navigate to the Involve Interpreter portal URL provided to you by Involve. The portal works best in a modern browser such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Safari.

### Activating Your Account
When your account is created by Involve, you will receive a welcome email containing an **Activate my account** button.

1. Click **Activate my account** in the email (the link expires after 7 days)
2. You will be taken to an activation page — enter a password of your choice
3. Click **Activate** to complete setup
4. You can now sign in using your email address and the password you chose

> If your activation link has expired, contact Involve and they can resend the welcome email.

---

## Signing In and Out

### Signing In
1. Go to the portal and enter your **email address** and **password**
2. Click **Sign in**

### Forgotten Password
1. On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address and click **Send reset link**
3. Check your inbox for a reset email and follow the link (expires after 24 hours)
4. Enter your new password and confirm

### Signing Out
Click your name or profile icon in the top right corner and select **Sign out**.

---

## The Dashboard

After signing in you will land on the **Dashboard**. This gives you an at-a-glance view of:

- **Upcoming appointments** — confirmed bookings coming up soon
- **Quick actions** — shortcuts to common tasks

Click any booking row to view its full details.

---

## Making a Booking

1. From the Dashboard, click **New booking**, or navigate to **New Booking** in the menu
2. Fill in the booking form:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Date | The date you need the interpreter |
| Time | The start time of the appointment |
| Duration | How long the appointment is expected to last |
| Booking type | The type of appointment (e.g. in-person, video) |
| Contact email | Email address for the point of contact |
| Contact number | Phone number for the point of contact |
| Video URL | If the appointment is online, paste the meeting link here |
| Additional information | Any other details that would help the interpreter |

3. Click **Create booking** when you are ready to submit

You will receive a confirmation email and be returned to the Dashboard. Your booking will appear as **Pending** until an interpreter is assigned.

---

## Viewing Your Bookings

Use the navigation menu to access your bookings by status:

- **My Bookings** — all bookings in one list
- **Pending Bookings** — submitted and awaiting an interpreter
- **Upcoming Bookings** — confirmed with an interpreter assigned
- **Cancelled Bookings** — bookings that have been cancelled

Click any row to expand the booking details, including the date, time, interpreter, and any video link.

---

## Editing a Booking

You can update a booking that has not yet been fulfilled:

1. Navigate to your bookings and click the booking you want to change
2. Click **Edit**
3. Update the relevant fields
4. Click **Save changes**

You will receive an email confirming the update.

---

## Cancelling a Booking

1. Navigate to your bookings and click the booking you want to cancel
2. Click **Edit**
3. Click **Cancel booking** (shown in orange)
4. Confirm by clicking **Yes, cancel booking**

You will receive a cancellation confirmation email. Cancelled bookings cannot be reinstated — please submit a new booking if you still require an interpreter.

---

## Your Profile

You can update your personal details at any time:

1. Click your name or profile icon in the top right corner
2. Select **Profile**
3. Update your first name, last name, or phone number
4. Click **Save changes**

You can also change your password from the same screen under the **Change password** section.

---

## Email Notifications

You will automatically receive emails when:

- Your booking is submitted
- Your booking is updated
- An interpreter is confirmed for your booking
- Your booking is cancelled
- Involve are unable to fulfil your booking request

---

## Getting Help

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact Involve directly. Do not share your password with anyone.
